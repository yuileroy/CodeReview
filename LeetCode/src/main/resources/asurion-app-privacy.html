An important note regarding Streaming Assistant

To enable you to onboard streaming services successfully and troubleshoot any issues in your home setup that impede your streaming, with your permission, an Asurion expert can perform a Home Network Check-Up. As part of the check-up, Asurion collects information about your home network to understand its capabilities (e.g., type of broadband connection, make and model of your router, and maximum download and upload capacity) and health (e.g., measured levels of congestion, packet loss, jitter, latency, and throughput). In addition, the check-up collects information about the streaming services available in your home and the devices using your home network (e.g., your device’s make and model, your device’s Wi-Fi signal strength from router, and the maximum speed with which your device can operate with respect to your router). The objective of the check-up is to identify any issues (e.g., a device using an unreasonably high amount of bandwidth) and make recommendations to you to enhance your in-home streaming.
